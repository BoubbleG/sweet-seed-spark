# Prompts de imagem — Point do Gordinho

Prompts prontos para gerar fotos de cada item do cardápio. Cada prompt descreve
todos os ingredientes visíveis para que a imagem corresponda exatamente ao que o
cliente recebe. Estilo padrão: food photography profissional, fundo de mesa de
madeira escura, iluminação quente lateral, enquadramento 3/4 close-up, sem texto,
sem logos, sem mãos.

---

## Hambúrgueres

### Burguer Simples
**Ingredientes:** pão brioche, 1 hambúrguer bovino, batata palha, cebola caramelizada, mussarela derretida, molho da casa.

**Prompt:**
> Professional food photography of a Brazilian-style burger on a dark wooden table. Toasted golden brioche bun (top and bottom), one thick juicy beef patty with grill marks, a generous layer of melted mozzarella cheese dripping over the patty, caramelized onions on top of the cheese, a sprinkle of crispy shoestring potato sticks (batata palha) visible at the edges, and a drizzle of creamy house sauce. 3/4 close-up angle, warm side lighting, shallow depth of field, no text, no logos, no hands.

---

### Duplo Cheddar
**Ingredientes:** pão brioche, **2 hambúrgueres bovinos**, bacon, cebola caramelizada, mussarela, cheddar especial.

**Prompt:**
> Professional food photography of a double cheddar burger on a dark wooden table. Toasted golden brioche bun, TWO thick beef patties stacked on top of each other with a slice of melted mozzarella between them, a generous pour of melted orange cheddar sauce cascading down both patties and the sides of the bun, two crispy bacon strips, and caramelized onions on top. The double-patty stack must be clearly visible from the side. 3/4 close-up angle, warm side lighting, glossy cheese pull, no text, no logos, no hands.

## Combos

### Combo Família
**Composição:** 4 hambúrgueres + 20 mini coxinhas + 1 porção de batata com cheddar e bacon + 1 refrigerante de 1 litro.

**Prompt:**
> Professional overhead food photography of a large Brazilian family combo on a dark wooden table. FOUR identical burgers (toasted brioche buns with beef patty, melted cheese and caramelized onions visible) arranged together, a generous pile of TWENTY golden mini coxinhas (small teardrop-shaped Brazilian fried chicken croquettes), one black basket of French fries smothered in melted orange cheddar sauce and crispy diced bacon, and one 1-liter Coca-Cola bottle. Composition shows every item clearly, warm overhead lighting, no hands.

---

### Combo Burguer Mac
**Composição:** 1 Burguer Mac (pão brioche, hambúrguer bovino, queijo, alface, molho especial) + 1 refrigerante lata 269ml.

**Prompt:**
> Professional food photography of a single Brazilian burger combo on a dark wooden table. One Burguer Mac (toasted brioche bun, beef patty, melted cheese, fresh shredded lettuce, special creamy sauce) next to one slim 269ml soda can (generic red can). Warm side lighting, 3/4 angle, both items clearly visible, no extra items, no hands.

---

### Combo Duplo Cheddar
**Composição:** 1 Duplo Cheddar (2 hambúrgueres, bacon, cebola caramelizada, mussarela, cheddar especial) + porção de batata frita + 1 refrigerante lata 350ml.

**Prompt:**
> Professional food photography of a Brazilian double cheddar burger combo on a dark wooden table. One double cheddar burger (toasted brioche bun, TWO stacked beef patties, melted mozzarella between them, cascading orange cheddar sauce, crispy bacon, caramelized onions), one black basket of golden crispy French fries, and one 350ml soda can. All three items clearly visible, warm side lighting, 3/4 angle, no hands.
